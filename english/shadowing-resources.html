Shadowing is a useful technique for improving your pronunciation. On this page, you will find links to resources, mostly YouTube videos, that you can use for shadowing native speakers of British English.

Resources Related to Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is a fantasy television series. In edition to the episodes themselves, there are also a number of short animated sequences under the title Histories and Lore. These videos are spoken by actors from the television series, who are mostly native speakers of Britis English. The actors use a variety of accents; not all of them are easy to “shadow”. The actors with the clearest pronunciation include Charles Dance (Tywinn Lannister), Jonathan Pryce (the High Sparrow), Iain Glen (Jorah Mormont), Harry Lloyd (Viserys Targaryen), Conleth Hill (Varys) and Mark Addy (Robert Baratheon).
