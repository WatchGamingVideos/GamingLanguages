Books and Sites about Language Learning Methods

Books are listed in reverse chronological order.

This book describes techniques for learning vocabulary and grammar, and for improving reading skills, writing skills, listening skilss and oral skills.

Wyner: Fluent Forever

Wyner, Gabriel: Fluent Forever: How to Learn Any Language Fast and Never Forget It. New York: Harmony Books, 2014.

This book explains a lot of cognitive psychology that is relevant to learning in general and learning languages specifically. It also gives very practical advices about techniques (e.g. spaced repetition) and tools.
The accompanying website provides lots of materials that complement the book.

Brown, Roediger & McDaniel: Make It Stick

Brown, Peter C.; Roediger, Henry L. III; McDaniel, Mark A.: Make It Stick: The Science of Successful Learning. Cambridge, MA, London, England: The Belknap Press of Harvard University Press, 2014.

This book is not specifically about learning languages but about learning in general.
