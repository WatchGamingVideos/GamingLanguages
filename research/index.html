Self-Regulated Learning

  • Oxford, Rebecca L.: Teaching & Researching: Language Learning Strategies. Routledge, 2010. ISBN 9780582381292.
    This book is heavily based on research. It present the Strategy Self-Regulation (S2R) Model of language learning, including practical applications of the model.

Language Learning Strategies

  • Oxford, Rebecca L.: Language Learning Strategies: What Every Teacher Should Know. Heinle, 1990. ISBN 978-0-8384-2862-7. (342 pages)
    This book is primarily for teachers of foreign languages at secondary, university and adult levels. It is mainly about teaching (language) learning strategies but the survey of language learning strategies may also be useful for learners.
  • Griffiths, Carol: The Strategy Factor in Successful Language Learning. Multilingual Matters, 2013. (240 pages) ISBN 9781847699404.
  • Hurd, Stella; Lewis, Tim, eds.: Language Learning Strategies in Independent Settings. Multilingual Matters, 2008. (352 pages) ISBN 9781847690975.
    This book is mainly based on research on language learning at the secondary level.
  • Cohen, Andrew D.: Strategies in Learning and Using a Second Language. 2nd edition. Routledge, 2010. (440 pages) ISBN 9781408253991.
    Another book aimed at language teachers that is based on research, for example, on strategies to learn Spanish grammar.
