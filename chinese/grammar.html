Websites and YouTube Videos on Chinese Grammar

  • Chinese Grammar Wiki by AllSet Learning, a consultancy that helps people learn Chinese. This is one of the most detailed online resource on Chinese grammar. (The content is available under a Creative Commons licence that does not allow commercial reuse.)
  • Li Tianming: A Handbook of Modern Chinese Grammar (draft; each chapter is available as a PDF file).
  • 汉语语法入门 An Introduction to Chinese Grammar on the Chinese Notes website, which is maintained by Nan Tien Institute, Australia. (You can find the website's source code on GitHub.)
  • Top 6 grammar mistakes made by Chinese learners on Chinlingo.com (22.12.2015).
  • Mandarin Essential Grammar (hosted by Yale University's Center for Language Study).
  • Gateway to Chinese: Gateway to Chinese Grammar Lessons: YouTube playlist with 20 short videos about elementary grammar, for example, compound verbs.
