Pronunciation

  • Lionel Hosxe: FRANCAIS - FLE - Exercices de diction (3-minute video with pronunciation exercices; the listener is expected to repeat sentences spoken by the teacher).
  • netprof: Exercices de diction (3-minute video with advanced pronunciation exercices; the listener is expected to repeat sentences spoken by the teacher).
  • netprof: Exercices de diction (4-minute video with advanced pronunciation exercices; the listener is expected to repeat some tongue-twisters spoken by the teacher).

Improving Your Voice

  • Gilles Pesnot: Sublimez le timbre de votre voix (11 minutes) and Sublimez (part 2) le timbre de votre voix (7 minutes) These two videos describe exercices for singers and public speakers.
  • Alexis Santin: L'homme qui TRANSFORMÉ ma voix + 1 exercice pour améliorer votre diction (5 minutes). Although the video claims to be about improving your voice, it only explains a technique to improve your articulation. (Most of the video is really about a voice coach that helped Alexis Santin.)
  • Enzo Duval: Comment avoir une belle voix : 3 exercices: a 6-minute video that explains three exercices that should make you more pleasant to listen to.

General Resources for Learning French

  • Französisch lernen mit Vincent: YouTube channel with hundreds of videos, many of which are organised in a few playlists, e.g. Phonetik, Konjugation, Grammatik, usw.. The website where these videos are used is Learn French with Vincent.
