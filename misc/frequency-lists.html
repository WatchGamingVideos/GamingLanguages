Frequency Lists

Frequency Lists for English

  • Davis, Mark; Gardner, Dee: A Frequency Dictionary of Contemporary American English: Word Sketches, Collocates and Thematic Lists. Routledge, 2010. (368 pages) ISBN 9780415490634.
    5,000 most frequently used words; based on data from a 385 million word corpus.
  • Quasthoff, Uwe; Fielder, Sabine; Hallsteinsdóttir, Erla: Frequency Dictionary English. Leipziger Universitätsverlag, 2012. (110 pages and a CD-ROM) ISBN 978-3-86583-654-0.
    Most frequent 1,000 words; the CD-ROM provides a longer list; no information about the size of the corpus upon which the frequency list is based.
  • Word frequency data: Corpus of Contemporary American English. 5,000-60,000 word lists, based on the 450 million word Corpus of Contemporary American English (COCA).
  • Audience Dialogue: Sustainable English - a vocabulary: list of the 15,000 commonest words, based on the 100 million word British National Corpus (BNC). See also Word frequencies in English.

Frequency Lists for Chinese Languages

  • Xiao, Richard; Rayson, Paul; McEnery, Tony: A Frequency Dictionary of Mandarin Chinese: Core Vocabulary for Learners. Routledge, 2009. (390 pages) ISBN 9780415455862.
    5,000 most common words and 2,000 most common simplified Chinese characters. Based on a fifty-million-word corpus composed of spoken, fiction, non-fiction and news texts in current use.

Frequency Lists for Romance Languages

  • Davies, Mark; Raposo Preto-Bay, Ana Maria: A Frequency Dictionary of Portuguese. Routledge, 2008. (342 pages). ISBN 9780415419970.
    5,000 most commonly used words. Based on a twenty-million-word collection of Portuguese (taken from both Portuguese and Brazilian sources).
  • Davies, Mark: A Frequency Dictionary of Spanish: Core Vocabulary for Learners. Routledge, 2006. (286 pages). ISBN 9780415334297.
    5,000 most commonly used words. Based on a twenty million word corpus evenly divided between spoken, fiction and non-fiction texts from both Spain and Latin America.

Frequency Lists for Slavic Languages

  • Sharoff, Serge; Umanskaya, Elena; Wilson, James: A Frequency Dictionary of Russian: Core Vocabulary for Learners. Routledge, 2013. (390 pages) ISBN 9780415521420.
    5,000 most commonly used words; 300 most frequent multiword constructions; based on data from a 150-million-word internet corpus taken from more than 75,000 webpages.
  • Quasthoff, Uwe; Fielder, Sabine; Hallsteinsdóttir, Erla: Frequency Dictionary Russian - Частотный словарь русского языка. Leipziger Universitätsverlag, 2017. (117 pages and a CD-ROM) ISBN 978-3-96023-103-5.
    Most frequent 1,000 words; the CD-ROM provides a longer list; no information about the size of the corpus upon which the frequency list is based.
  • Quasthoff, Uwe; Fielder, Sabine; Hallsteinsdóttir, Erla: Frequency Dictionary Ukrainian - Частотний словник української мови. Leipziger Universitätsverlag, 2016. (125 pages and a CD-ROM) ISBN 978-3-96023-008-3.
    Most frequent 1,000 words; the CD-ROM provides a longer list; no information about the size of the corpus upon which the frequency list is based.
